My favorite Article is given by Lord Krishna. It's not just an article, but words given by Lord Krishna to a great warrior, Arjun. It is about our duty.

" कर्म यर्म ेवाधिकार ते मा फलेषुकदाचन;
मा कर्मफलह र्म ेतर्भूु र्मा र्भू ते स गोऽ वकर्मणिर्म । "

Meaning: "You have the right to perform your actions, but you are not entitled to the fruits of the actions. Do not let the fruit be the purpose of your actions, and therefore you won't be attached to not doing your duty."
